About My World of Text

{% endblock %} {% block content %}

My World of Text is a hobby-based, self-hosted recreation of FP/system2k's NodeWorldofText. It is an infinitely large collaborative text canvas where anyone can write anywhere, at any time. I created this instance so I can freely experiment and add features that the original OWOT/YWOT may not have.

You can create or explore worlds simply by adding any letters to the end of the URL. For example, {{ website }}forexample. Every world begins blank, and you can also create a custom world to share with friends or communities.

This site is community-influenced. I am open to feature suggestions, improvements, and experimental ideas. Join the community and help shape the direction of the site:
